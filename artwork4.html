THE KIND KING | Plotagraph

The concept consists on the idea that people can sometimes feel like they are trapped in their armor making it difficult to express any feelings.

A plotagraph is an image that contains elements that create the illusion of movement. It is often used on visual narratives with the obective of achieving a more immersive experience for the viewer. Using photoshop I was able to duplicate parts of the image in multiple layers with the purpose of creating a sequence. Said sequence would play in a loop creating the illusion of movement. This was achieved by creating a video timeline with multiple clips. By using the “smart object” function, I blended the elements into one final animated image.
